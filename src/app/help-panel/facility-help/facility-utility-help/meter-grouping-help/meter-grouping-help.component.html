Meter Grouping

The utility meters can be grouped by utility type or user preference.

These groups represent your aggregated meter data for use in Visualization and Analysis. For reporting, they will be grouped by utility type and source (electricity, natural gas, etc.) or emissions scope.

Meters can be put into groups by dragging the meter from one group to another or by clicking the "" for the meter. The "" can also move meters between utility types (Energy, Water) to Other or back (depending on the utility source of the meter). The "Ungrouped" group will disappear once you have assigned all the meters to a designated group.

The date range can be changed to adjust the data shown in the tables and graphs.

The tables show the calendarized meter groups.

The graph will show monthly cost and utility consumption. Either as bars or lines depending on what is selected.

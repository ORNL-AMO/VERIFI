Meter Grouping

The utility meters can be grouped by utility type or user preference.

These groups will represent your data visualization and regression analysis.

These group can be edited by dragging the meter from one group to another. The “Ungrouped” group will disappear once you have assigned all the meters to a designated group.

The date range can be changed to adjust the data shown in the tables and graphs

The tables show the calendarized meter groups

The graph will show monthly cost and utility consumption. Either as bars or lines depending on what is selected.

Meter Grouping

The utility meters can be grouped by utility type or user preference.

These groups represent your aggregated meter data for use in Visualization and Analysis. For reporting, they will be grouped by utility type and source (electricity, natural gas, etc.) or emissions scope.

There are three types of groups: Energy, Water, and Other. The Energy group will contain all the energy meters (electricity, natural gas, etc.) and the Water group will contain all the water meters. The Other group can be used for all other meters including energy and water meters.

Clicking " Add New Group" will allow you to create a new group. You can also edit existing groups by clicking the "" button or clicking the group name. This will allow you to change the group name and the meters selected to be a member of that group.

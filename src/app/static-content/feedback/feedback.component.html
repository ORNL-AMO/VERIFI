Feedback

We are always looking to improve Verifi and get feedback on accuracy, usability, presentation or new features. Please take our survey on Survey Monkey: https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/DOE-AMO-TOOLS. Thank you for your feedback!


Bug Reporting

To report a bug or otherwise provide feedback, please email Kristina Armstrong at armstrongko@ornl.gov. Please include your operating system (e.g., Windows 10, Mac OS 10.12), a screenshot of the issue and download your current data set so that we can understand the problem.

You are using an international version of the application, please specify that when reporting bugs.

Thank you for your feedback!

Calendarization

View and manage how your metered data is calendarized, the process of standardizing the time periods for reporting and analysis purposes.

Click "Select Method" to view more information on how and why to calendarize your data.

Select "Do Not Calendarize Meter Data" to have readings apply to the month they are entered in.

Select "Calendarize Meter Data" to adjust data so that the reporting periods align with calendar months.

Select "Evenly Distribute Data Annually" to distribute data evenly across the year a bill is entered in.

Use the "Filter" button to view data from a specific time range.

Use the "Table/Graph" options to view your data in a table or time series format.

Use the "Site/Source" energy options to view your data in site or source units.

The " Copy Table" button will copy the table data to your clipboard that can then be pasted into an excel file.

The "" button will export all of the meters and their data to VERIFI's excel template. This can be used as a backup or as a way to send data to others to be imported.
